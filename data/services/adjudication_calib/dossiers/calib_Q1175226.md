> MACPHERSON, THE HON. SIR DAVID LEWIS, K.C.M.G. (1884).—A member of the Queen's privy council for Canada, and speaker of the senate till 1883. Educated at the Royal Academy in that town; was elected for the electoral division of Saugnee; to the legislative council of the former province of Canada; and on the confederation of the provinces of British North America, was summoned by royal mandamus to a seat in the senate; acted as minister of the interior during Sir John A. Macdonald's absence from Canada, 1881, resigned 1885.
