> RICHARDS, HON. ALBERT NORTON, Q.C.—Born, 1822; called to the bar of Upper Canada, 1848; to that of British Columbia, 1871; created a Q.C., 1863; sat in legislative assembly, Canada, from 1868 to 1864, and in house of commons from 1872 until January, 1874; was a member of the executive council, Canada, and solicitor-general for Upper Canada, 1863-4; attorney-general for Rupert's Land, and the north-west territories, October, 1869; legal adviser in British Columbia for the dominion government, 1874; lieutenant-governor of the province of British Columbia, 20th July, 1876, to 19th July, 1881.
