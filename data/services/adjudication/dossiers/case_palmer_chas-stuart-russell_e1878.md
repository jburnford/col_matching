> PALMER, CHAS. STUART RUSSELL, M.I.C.E.—Ed. at Roorkee Civil Eng. Coll., India; ent. P.W.D., India, Oct., 1878, as asst. engrnr.: spec. duty in England, 1886 to 1887; asst. to ch. engrnr. and asst. sec. to ch. comanr., cent. provs., up to 1889, when apptd. exec. engrnr. of Hoochangabad div., and in 1892 of Nagpur div.; ret. from Indian serv., May, 1894; asst. survr., W. Aust., Oct., 1894; engrnr. in charge, roads and bridges, and harbours and rivers branches, Aug., 1897, of water supply, 1898, and sewerage, 1900; engrnr.-in-chief, June, 1902; inspct. engrnr. for W. A. in Lond., 1904.
