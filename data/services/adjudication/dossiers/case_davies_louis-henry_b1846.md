> DAVIES, HON. SIR LOUIS HENRY, K.C.M.G. (1897).—B. 1846; called to the bar, 1866; Q.C. in 1880; was one of the Canadian counsel before the internat. fishery comsn. at Halifax in 1877; solr. gen. of P.E. Is., 1869, and again in 1872-3; led the opposition in P.E. Is. Legislature until Sept., 1876, when he became premier and atty.-gen.; resig. in Mar., 1879, and was defeated at the gen. elections which followed; was first returned to the House of Commons at the gen. elections in 1882, and has since sat for Queen's; min. of marine and fisheries in Sir W. Laurier's govt., June, 1896; mem. joint high comsnr., Quebec and Washington, 1898-9; puisne judge of sup. court, 1901.
