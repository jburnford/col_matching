> LYONS, CAPT. F. W.—Ed. at Portarlington school, and Trin. Coll., Dublin; joined 80th regt., as 2nd lieut., 15th Feb., 1878; capt., 1884; adjutant, 1885; adjut. of volunteers, 1887-90; captain, reserve of officers, 1890; J.P., Straits Settlements, 1891; ag. asst. aupt. of pol., S.S., 5th Mar., 1891, to 31st Mar., 1893; apptd. ast. comsnr., Perak Sikhs, 1st Apr., 1893; acted as capt., supt. of pol., and prisons, Selangor, Aug., 1893 to Feb., 1895; served in S. Africa, 1878-80, medal with clasp; served in Soudan, 1885-86, medal and Khedive's Star; served in Pahang during disturbances, and recd. thanks of S. of S., 1894; acted as chief pol. offr., Perak, 1900-2; apptd. deputy sup. of pol., Hong Kong, 18th Sept., 1902; J.P., Hong Kong, 1902; ag. capt. supt. of pol., etc., 1903-4, and 1907-8; M.L.C., 1911.
