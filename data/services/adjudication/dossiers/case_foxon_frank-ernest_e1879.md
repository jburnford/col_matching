> FOXON, FRANK ERNEST.—3rd class clk. civ. serv., Natal, and 2nd clk. and Zulu interp., Umgani div., Nov., 1880; ag. clk. of ct., regis. circuit ct., and sub-distributor of stamps, Weenen country, 1886; clk. of ct., dep.-clk. of peace, and sub-distributor of stamps, Upper Umkomangvi div., May, 1887; admnstr. native law, Impendelele, Mar., 1888; ditto, Inanda Location, Jan., 1890; ag. mag., Lower Tugela div., Apr. to June, 1890; ag. R.M. at Verulam and Stanger, 1890 to 1894; R.M. for col., and mag., Ndwedwe div., July, 1894; J.P. for col., 1893; served at commencement of Zulu war, 1879, in imp. transport dept., afterwards as lieut. Natal N. pioneers (medal and clasp); served as levy leader in command of Siebepp's contingent and Yamela's mounted men against Dinuzulu in Zululand, 1888; lieut., Natal carbineers, Apl., 1889, capt., Dec., 1897; mag., Ixopo div., 1897; served through the Boer war, 1899-1900, including the siege of Ladysmith; mag., Estcourt div., 1905; mag., Pietermaritzburg div., Nov., 1915.
