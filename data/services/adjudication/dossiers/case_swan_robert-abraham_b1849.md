**Version `col1883-L29668.v` — printed in editions [1883, 1886, 1888, 1889, 1890, 1894, 1896, 1898, 1899, 1900, 1905, 1906, 1907, 1908, 1909, 1910, 1911, 1912, 1913]:**

> SWAN, ROBERT ABRAHAM.—B. 1849; matric. at Madras Univ., Dec., 1868; passed special law test prescribed for uncoventional supts. and asst.-supts. of pol.; 2nd class inspr. of pol. in the Cuddapah dist., Madras, May, 1870; in Aug., 1871, 1st class; May, 1874, passed in Telugu; Oct., 1876, special asst. to sub-collr. and joint mag. of Cuddapah for famine relief duty, and in same month apptd. mag. with 1st class powers, and placed in charge of the Kadri and Madnapully Talugs of Cuddapah dist.; in Feb., 1877, stip. mag. Mauritius; Jan., 1878, stip. mag. of Flacq; and in Aug., 1879, Grand Port and Savanne; mem. of consnr. to frame reguls. under "labour ordinance of 1878"; member of Lincoln's Inn, 1882; called to the bar, 1887; stip. mag. Br. Guiana, Nov., 1883; in July, 1884, sent on special duty to Madras to organise an emigr. agency for Br. Guiana; sheriff, Essequibo co., Dec., 1887; chmn. lands titles inquiry consnr., Feb., 1890; a revising barrister under Br. Guiana Constit. Order, 1891; sheriff of Berbice and mag. New Amsterdam, 1892; acted as pol. mag., Georgetown, Feb., 1890, and Oct., 1894, to Apr., 1895; mem. of consnr. on poor-relief, 6th Apr., 1900; chairman of ditto, 7th Sept., 1900; appt. as pol. mag., Georgetown, confirmed by S. of S., 1st Oct., 1900; ag. puisne judge, sup. ct., 1st Oct., 1900; puisne judge, Trinidad, 3rd Aug., 1903; ag. ch. just., Trinidad and Tobago, 7th July to 12th Oct., 1904, and 26th June to 30th Oct., 1906; 1st puisne judge, Oct., 1906.

**Version `col1897-L34738.v` — printed in editions [1897]:**

> SWAN, Robert A.—Matriculated at Madras University, Dec., 1868; passed the special test prescribed for unencumbered superintendents and assistant-superintendents of police; 2nd class inspector of police in the Cuddapah division, Madras, May, 1870; in Aug., 1871, first class, May, 1874, passed in Telugu; in Oct., 1874, special assistant to the sub-collector and magistrate of Cuddapah for famine relief; and in the same month was appointed magistrate, and placed in charge of the Kurnool and Mahanagari Taluks of the Cuddapah division; in Feb., 1877, stipendiary magistrate, Me.
