> BUCK, HON. EDWARD CLARKE, M.L.C.E., M.I. Mech. E., M. Am. Soc. C.E., F.G.S., F.R.G.S.—Ed. Whitgift Gram. Schl., Croydon, England; employed on surveys, mining and drainage, Victoria, 1894-95; asst. engrnr., P.W.D., Perth, W. Aust., 1895; asst. engrnr. and res. engrnr. to Messrs. J. Walker and Sons, building Cape govt. rlyws., 1898 to 1902; during a period of the S. African war was attached to Royal Engineers; asst. city engrnr., Pretoria, 1902; asy. engrnr. in 1903; water wks. engrnr., 1903; dep. city engrnr. and water wks. engrnr., 1904-1908; consulting engrnr. to several London companies, 1908-1910; Trinidad partner to Messrs. Thompson and Hunter, consulting engrnr., 1910-1913; J.P. for Trinidad and Tobago; colonial civil engrnr., B. Guiana, 6th Nov., 1913; designation changed to dir. of pub. wks., Aug., 1914; offl. mem. of policy and J.P. for B. Guiana; seconded as director of sea defences, 1st Feb., 1916, but also in charge of irrigation works and surveys and artesian well-boring operations; lecturer and examiner of colonial candidates for certifs. issued by Royal Sanitary Inst., London.
