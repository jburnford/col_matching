> FLYNN, HON. EDMUND JAMES B.—B. 1847; ed. Quebec Seminary and Laval Univ. (L.L.B., 1873); admitted to Queb. bar, 1873; prof. of Roman law in Laval Univ., 1874-1914; I.L.D., Laval 1878; is dean of law faculty; practised as advoc. in Queb. City; mem., Queb. legislature for Gaspé, 1878-1900 when elected for Nicolet Co.; commnr., crown lands, Chapleau admtn., 1879-1882; commnr. of rly.s., and solr. gen., Ross admtn., 1884-87; commnr., crown lands, de Boucherville govt. from 1891-96; prime min. and commnr. of pub. wks., May, 1896 to May, 1897; leader of opposition, 1897 to 1904; retired from Queb. legislature 1904; Q.C. by Dom. govt., 1887 subsequently by Queb. govt.; bataillonier of Queb. bar, 1907-09; apptd. judge of superior ct., June, 1914; promoted to ct. of king’s bench, July, 1920.
