Prov., 16th-25th May, 1907; dist. comsnr., Meko, W. Prov., and offr. in charge of preventive service, 20th July to 27th Nov., 1908; ag. sen. astt. col. sec., Nov.-Dec., 1908; ag. ch. astt. col. sec., 1st Jan., 1909; apptd. to represent S. Nigeria in delimitation of Anglo-French (Nigeria-Dahomey) boundy., 13th Jan., 1909, and of Ekiti-boundy. (with N. Nigeria), 24th Feb., 1909; has done lit. work of various kinds, including complete verbal cross-index (40,000 slips) to Yule's "Glos. of Ang.-Indian Words"; article on "Igara land" in "Blackwood's Mag.", Sept., 1904; "Cross River Natives," Jan., 1905.
