Temp., 1871; J.P., Barbados, 1873; coroner for parishes of Christchurch and St. George, 1873; ag. provost-marshal and serjt.-at-arms, 1874; ag. pol. mag. of St. Michael's parish, 1875; ag. judge of astt. ct. of appeal, 1875-7-9; comanr. of probates, 1877; ag. solr.-gen., 1880; regir. of friendly and building societies, 1880; ag. judge of the astt. ct. of appeal, 1880-82; ag. atty.-gen. and ex-officio mem. exec. and legis. couns.; ch. just. (on two occasions for special purposes) and chancellor of the diocese; sole comanr. to report on a complaint of the vestry of St. Michael (Barbados) against Bridgetown Water Works Co.; comanr. to report on working of courts of inferior juris. (Barbados); comanr. to investigate charges against rioters (Barbados); Grenada, 1883, judge of astt. ct. of appeal, 1883; ag. ch. just., St. Vincent, 1884-5; local comanr. of incumbered estates act. (St. Vincent), Oct., 1884; comanr. to examine and report on the govt. schl., St. Vincent; comanr. to perform special duty in Grenada at the request of S. of S.; res. mag., Jamaica, 1888.
