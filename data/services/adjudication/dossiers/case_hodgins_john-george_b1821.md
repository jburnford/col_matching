> HODGINS, JOHN GEORGE, M.A., LL.D., F.R.G.S., Deputy Minister of Education for Ontario, Toronto.—Born, Dublin, 1821; educated at Upper Canada Academy and Victoria College, Cobourg, M.A.; 1856; LL.B., Toronto, 1860, and LL.D., 1870; called to the bar of Ontario, 1870. Secy. of bd. of edn., Upper Canada, 1846; deputy head of dept. of edn., June, 1855; is an extensive writer on educational topics, and for over twenty years was the chief editor of the U.C. "Journal of Education," and author of numerous school books.
