> CROSS, J. W.—Clk., Zulu interp., and sub-distributor of stamps in the res. mag.'s office, Ixopo, Natal, 23rd Oct., 1876; dep. clk. of the peace for Ixopo, 2nd Mar., 1877; and on the 3rd Mar., 1879, apptd. in command of the Ixopo, Natal, native foot contingent, 800 strong; and subsequently transf'd. to command of the mounted contingent during the Zulu war; acted on several occasions as res. mag. of Ixopo; clk. to R.M. Weenen, July, 1886; admstr. of native law, Pagadi's location, 1889; mag., Lower Tugela div., 1894; R.M., Alfred div., 1896; mag., Lion River div., 1898; Umvoti div., 1904; Newcastle div., 1907; regist. and Zulu interp., special treason ct., 1908; mag., Dundee dist., 1911.
