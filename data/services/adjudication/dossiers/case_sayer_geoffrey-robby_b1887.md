> SAYER, GEOFFREY ROBBY, B.A.—B. 1887; ed. at Highgate schl. (foundationer) and Queen's Coll., Oxford; open exhibitioner in classics and Fitzgerald exhibitions; 2nd cls. class. mods.; 3rd cls. Lit. Hum. (B.A., 1910); cadet, Hong Kong, 24th Oct., 1910; pa.sed in Cantonese, Nov., 1912; ag. astt. dist. offr. (South), Nov., 1912 to Feb., 1913, and from Mar. to Nov., 1913; ag. astt. sup't. of police, 22nd Nov., 1913 to Oct., 1914; ag. dep. offr. recvr., Oct., 1914 to Mar., 1915; ag. chief astt. sec. for Chinese affair during Nov., 1914 and Aug., 1915, in addition to other duties; priv. sec. to gov', in addition to other duties, Jan. to Nov., 1915; attached to crown solr.'s off., Apr. to Sept., 1915; passed final exam. in Hakka, July, 1915; ag. chief astt. sec. for Chinese affair, Sept., 1915; on mily. serv., 1915-19; dep. registr. and appraiser, Hong Kong, Jan., Mar., 1920; head of sany. dept., Mar., 1920; passed in Mandarin, May, 1921.
