Pacifico, Dec., 1921; seconded for sp. serv. with the W. Pacific High Comsnr. to preside over certain murder trials in the Solomon Is., Dec., 1921 to Mar., 1922; res. mag., Jamaica, July, 1922; called to bar, Gray's Inn, May, 1925; solr.-gen., Kenya, Apr., 1927; ag. atty.-gen. in 1932 and 1933; King's proctor, Dec., 1928; puisne judge and mem., W. African ct. of appl., Gold Coast, Jan., 1937.
