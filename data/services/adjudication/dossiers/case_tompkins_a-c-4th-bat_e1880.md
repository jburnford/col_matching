> TOMPKINS, A.C., Lieut.-Col.—Ed. privately, and at Versailles, Paris and Hasings; served in 6th Inniskilling Dragoons, 1880-81; 2nd lieut. and lieut., Roy. Westmorland Militia, 1881-82; served with 1st and 2nd batts., The Black Watch (Roy. Highrs.), The Roy. Horse Guards (Blues), 1882-84; lieut., 4th Roy. Fus., 1884; sub-inspr., B. Honduras constab., Dec., 1887; inspr. and 2nd in command Frontier Defence Force, 1888-89; J.P. and dist. mag.; capt., Roy. Fus., 1889; commd. a troop of the B.S.A. Co.'s pol. in pioneer exped., Mashonaland, 1889-91; inspr., Sierra Leone Frontier Force, Aug., 1892; sub-inspr.-gen., S. Leone Frontier Force, and ag. inspr.-gen., 1893: J.P., S. Leone, 1893; invalided and resig., Dec., 1893; apptd. under F.O., and seconded for serv. under the Egyptian govt. as adjt-major and major, Coast Guard serv. (Camel Corps, Cavalry, Infy. and Marine); comdt., Western Frontier dist., Mex., 1894-5; Port Said and Canal dist., 1896-7; rod. thanks of H.H. the Khedive and Egyptian govt. for sp. preventive serv., Port Said, 1896; comdt., Suez and Red Sea dist., 1897-8; supply and transport offr. during survey and constrn. of state rly. to Lake Victoria Nyanza, E. Africa and Uganda, 1899-1901; major, Roy. Fus., 1899; field offr., ag. adjutant and musketry instr., 10th Prov. Batt., R. Sussex, R. Dublin Fusiliers and Leinster Regts., 1901; granted rank of col. by H.M. King Edward VII. to command mil., pol., and vol. forces of the Sultan of Johore, 1901-06; lieut.-col., Roy. Fus., 1905; attd. as 2nd in command, 1st Roy. Fus., and 5th Roy. Fus., 1907; dir. of transport, Cape to Cairo sur. exped., 1907-08; passed mukl. and vety. courses, Hythe and Aldershot, 1908; on sp. serv. as dir. of transport (land and water) under Turkish govt., Mesopotamia, 1909-11; on staff of the Lord Chamberlain, St. James' Palace, 1911-12 (coronation med.); on sp. serv. in W. Africa, 1913; on sp. serv. in Egypt, Sudan and Equatorial Provs., Red Sea Littoral, Aden, Ceylon, 1914; served throughout the great war, Aug., 1914 to 1918, on western front, viz.:—remount staff, B.E.F., 1914; 2nd in command, serv. batt., Roy. Fus., R.E.F., 1915; A.P.M., 12th div., B.E.F., 1915-16; award French Order of Cross of Merit (officer), 1916; adminis. comdt., 5th army railheads, B.E.F., 1917 (graded D.A.Q.M.G.); wounded, Nov., 1917; adminis. comdt., 4th army railheads, B.E.F. 1918; twice ment. in descp., 1916-17; adminis. comdt., 3rd army railheads, promoted full col. and comdt. (graded A.A.G.), sub-areas in France and Belgium, Apr. to Nov., 1919; dist. comanr., Famagusta, Cyprus, 30th Sept., 1920; comanr., Famagusta and Paphos dists., sheriff, prov. marshall, coroner, chmn., dist. hosps., etc., 1921-22.
