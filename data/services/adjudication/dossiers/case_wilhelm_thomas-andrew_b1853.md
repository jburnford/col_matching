> WILHELM, THOMAS ANDREW.—B. 1853; entd. col. serv., S. Leone, 10th May, 1878; ag. librarian at govt. house for different periods, 1878-1888; clk., treas., 19th Aug., 1881; accompanied Govr. Pinkett to Talliah war, 1882-1883; ag. 2nd elk., col. secretariat, 1887; ag. ch. elk., ditto, 1887; priv. sec. and accompanied Govr. Rowe on numerous expedts. to the Hinterland, between 1878 and 1888; 2nd elk., registr.-gen.'s dept., 1st Oct., 1888; ag. ch. elk., ditto, and dep. registr. of births and deaths, 1888-1889, and Jan. to July, 1890; ch. elk., off. of mast. of sup. ct. and registr.-gen., 1890; dep. registr.-gen., 3rd Aug., 1890; dep. registr. of births and deaths, Freetown dist., 30th Aug., 1890; asst. inspr. of wks. and roads, survey dept., 1st Feb., 1891; acted at various times as offr. in charge of survey dept., 1891-1894; offr. in charge, survey dept., May to Oct., 1894; ditto merchants' powder magazine, May to Oct., 1894; ch. draughtsman, survey dept., 15th Mar., 1895; offr. in charge of pub. wks., Moyamba, Mar. to June, 1899; ch. draughtsman, rly. wharf extension scheme, Mar. to Dec., 1901; ag. survr. of Crown lands, July to Sept., 1904.
