**Version `col1883-L28727.v` — printed in editions [1883]:**

> MOIR, Alexander Wilson, C.M.G. (1877).—In customs department, Jamaica, February, 1848; presiding magistrate of the (late) colony of Bay Islands in Dec. 1855; public treasurer of Honduras, July, 1860, but at the urgent request of the Spanish Honduras government, was continued at his post in the Bay Islands, until the cession of the colony to the republic on the 1st June, 1861; acting colonial secretary with a dormant commission as superintendent at the same place in June, 1862; president of the Turks and Caicos Island in Aug., 1862; president of the Virgin Islands, Oct., 1869; president of Dominica, June, 1871; president of St. Kitts, Feb., 1872; a member of the executive council of the Leeward Islands (1871), and a nominated member of the general council.
